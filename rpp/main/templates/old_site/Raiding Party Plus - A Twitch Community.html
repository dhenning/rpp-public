Mission Statement

RaidingPartyPlus raids small streamers to help reach the affiliate goals, and to bring them into the community. After a new accession has joined the ranks, we continue to give love and support to the members channel with many perks as they climb up the embedded rank structure.

Vision Statement

The vision of RaidingPartyPlus is to actively be of service to its members and the Twitch community as pillar of acceptance, inspiration, and support. We will strive together to build a society that every member, whether streamer or fan, can call home. To accomplish this endeavour, it is agreed upon that every person whether a new recruit or a practiced veteran, continually seek out new ways to aid their fellow constituents. That which a single person aims for becomes that which the group seeks out to complete.
